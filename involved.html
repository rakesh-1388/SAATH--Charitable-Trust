Internships at Saath

Saath Charitable Trust offers immersive internship opportunities for students and professionals interested in urban development and social change. Interns contribute to real-world projects across education, health, livelihoods, and other areas gaining hands-on experience with community-based initiatives.

To apply for internship at Saath you need to share the following to mail@saath.org:

  • Filled Form
  • Your updated resume
  • Letter from your University/College/Institute

Download Internship Form Please Click Here
Internship Form - Fill it and email it back to us at mail@saath.org

While applying for the internship please take note of the following

  • The subject line for the email should be – Internship at Saath | <Your name> | <University name>
  • All the documents mentioned need to be attached with the email for consideration of your application
  • The documents submitted by you will be shared with the Director, Internship Coordinator and senior management for consideration
  • Saath does not offer stipend for internships
  • Saath does not provide housing facilities for interns
  • Field work will be compulsory for all interns
  • Saath does not offer virtual/online internships
  • Applicants are required to contact Saath atleast 15 days before their expected joining date
  • The duration of internship at Saath should not be less than 15 days
  • Every intern at Saath is required to submit their weekly work report to their mentor
  • Upon completion of your internship at Saath, you will receive a digital certificate
  • Saath will not be liable for any accidents or injuries that happen during field visits

Volunteering at Saath

Volunteering at Saath is an opportunity to create real impact in the lives of urban poor communities. Whether you have a few hours, days, or weeks to contribute, your time and skills can make a meaningful difference. Volunteers support a range of our programs—be it teaching children, mentoring micro-entrepreneurs, supporting health and awareness drives, or contributing to creative campaigns and documentation.

We welcome individuals, student groups, and professionals from diverse backgrounds who are passionate about social change. Our team ensures that volunteers are engaged in roles that match their interests and strengths. Volunteering can be both in-person and virtual, based on program needs and your availability.

If you’d like to be a part of our mission, reach out to us at mail@saath.org. Let’s work together to build inclusive, resilient, and equitable communities.
